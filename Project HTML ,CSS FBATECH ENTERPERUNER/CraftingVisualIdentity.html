Photography

Overview of the Lecture

During the session with the talented photographer Iyad Ghwaneh, we covered several essential topics in photography, ranging from fundamental concepts to practical editing techniques.

Workshop Objectives

  • Photography using various cameras: Including mobile phones and professional cameras.
  • Camera types: How to choose the right one for your needs and lifestyle.
  • Basic photography settings: Understanding key adjustments for better shots.
  • Photography techniques and elements: Exploring techniques for capturing creative images.
  • Types of photography: Characteristics of various styles and the benefits of each approach.
  • Importance of lenses: Understanding lens types, such as the 30mm prime lens, ideal for product photography.
  • Editing with Lightroom: Utilizing Adobe Lightroom for post-processing and enhancing photos.

Topics Covered

  • Adobe Lightroom Editing: Iyad demonstrated how to edit photos using Adobe Lightroom. He showcased live editing on a photo he took, illustrating how adjustments in exposure, contrast, and color grading can transform an image.
  • Photography Techniques: We learned about different photography styles, including:
      Abstract Photography
      Street Photography
      Product Photography
  • Lens Recommendations: For product photography, Iyad emphasized the use of a 30mm prime lens. This lens has a fixed focal length with no zoom capabilities, making it perfect for capturing detailed shots with a strong focus on composition.

    • Conclusion

    The lecture provided a comprehensive understanding of essential photography concepts and practical tips for achieving better results, particularly in product and street photography.

    Graphic Design

    We had an insightful lecture on graphic design led by the skilled designer Ahmed Al-Saidin. The session covered the basics of design, visual inspiration, and practical applications.

    Topics Covered

    • Design Principles: An introduction to fundamental design concepts that help create visually appealing content.
    • Visual Inspiration: Learning how to cultivate visual inspiration to enhance creativity.
    • Print Test Software: We explored the Print Test software for practical design testing.
    • Logo Design: Techniques for designing a logo from scratch, focusing on simplicity and conveying impactful ideas.
    • Color Palettes: Understanding the importance of selecting the right color palettes for different design projects.

    Conclusion

    The workshops provided a comprehensive understanding of both photography and graphic design, equipping us with practical skills and creative techniques for future projects.
